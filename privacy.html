Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Information We Collect

CalibFlow collects information you provide directly when you create an account, register equipment, or contact support. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, company name, phone number, and password
  • Equipment Data: Device names, serial numbers, calibration dates, intervals, locations, and associated certificates
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, and interaction timestamps
  • Communications: Messages sent through our support channels

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, maintain, and improve CalibFlow's calibration management services
  • Send calibration deadline alerts via email and/or WhatsApp
  • Generate calibration certificates and compliance reports
  • Communicate with you about your account, updates, and support requests
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

3. Data Security

CalibFlow uses industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • Database: Supabase (PostgreSQL) with Row Level Security (RLS) — each company's data is completely isolated
  • In Transit: All data is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS 1.3
  • At Rest: AES-256 encryption for stored data
  • Access Control: Role-based access with admin, QA manager, technician, and viewer permissions

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only with:

  • Service Providers: Supabase (database), Resend (email delivery), PDFMonkey (certificate generation), Stripe (payments)
  • Calibration Vendors: Only when you explicitly schedule a calibration through our platform
  • Legal Requirements: When required by law or to protect our rights

5. Data Retention

We retain your data for the duration of your account's active status. Calibration records and certificates are stored indefinitely to maintain audit compliance. You may request data deletion by contacting privacy@calibflow.io.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data in standard formats (CSV, PDF)
  • Withdraw consent for optional communications

7. Cookies

CalibFlow uses essential cookies for authentication and session management. We do not use advertising or third-party tracking cookies.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or through an in-app notification.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:
privacy@calibflow.io
